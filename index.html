TRICYPAY Web System

System Analyst · Developer · UX/UI Designer

June 2025 – December 2025

Project Overview

TRICYPAY is a web-based transportation management system developed for the Tricycle Regulatory Unit (TRU) and the Public Safety and Traffic Management Unit (PSTMU). The system centralizes tricycle registration, transaction records, and traffic violation management, enabling LGUs to modernize operational workflows and ensure transparency and accountability.

Key Features

  • Centralized tricycle and driver registration management
  • Digital transaction and fare monitoring system
  • Traffic violation reporting, tracking, and resolution
  • Role-based access for LGUs, operators, and enforcement units
  • Integrated data synchronization across web, mobile, and IoT devices

System Impact

The system improved data accuracy, reduced manual processing, and enhanced coordination between government units. It provided LGUs with reliable insights for monitoring operations and enforcing transportation regulations.

Technologies Used

React · Node.js · Supabase · PostgreSQL · Agile · Arduino · ESP32
